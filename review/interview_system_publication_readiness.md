One gap: infrastructure. A candidate who uses this system alongside Part 6 of the Desk Bible would be well-prepared. But the Interview System alone cannot serve as a standalone resource because it never tests whether the candidate understood Part 6.

## 7. Publication Blockers

### Blocker 1: Infrastructure Content (CRITICAL)

Without infrastructure interview content, the publication cannot claim to be a comprehensive structured products interview guide. Any reviewer with desk experience will immediately note the absence of convention questions, P&L Explain, reserves, and operations content.

**Resolution**: Generate infrastructure interview content sourced from Part 6 §6.1-§6.11.

### Blocker 2: Role Coverage (CRITICAL)

With only 5 of 11 desk roles served, the publication excludes the majority of structured products hiring. PC and Ops are the two largest hiring functions.

**Resolution**: Add PC and Ops mock interview tracks.

### Blocker 3: Termsheet Exercises (CRITICAL)

Termsheet reading is the single most universal structured products interview exercise. Its absence is conspicuous.

**Resolution**: Add 3-5 termsheet reading exercises.

### Blocker 4: Part 6 Cross-References (HIGH)

The Interview System cannot be a standalone resource because it references the Desk Bible extensively. Without Part 6 references, readers who encounter infrastructure questions in real interviews have no link back to the teaching material.

**Resolution**: Add §6.x cross-references.
