## 1. What the Matrix Is

49 products × 10 dimensions. Sortable, filterable cross-family comparison. Dimensions: Complexity, Yield, Capital Protection, Credit Exposure, Liquidity, Path Dependency, Volatility Sensitivity, Correlation Sensitivity, Client Type, Market Environment.

~5,000 words (rubrics + commentary). ~12 pages. 7 visual assets.

## 4. Recommendation

### **Option C: Digital Supplement — with print summary in V1**

**Hybrid approach:**

1. **Full interactive matrix** → Digital Supplement. Sortable, filterable, 10 dimensions. This is where the matrix delivers maximum value — no print layout can match interactive filtering.

2. **2-page summary matrix** → Volume 1, Part 6 (after DNA Atlas). Simplified 49-row table showing 4 key dimensions only: Complexity, Yield, Capital Protection, Client Type. Fits on two pages (landscape orientation). Gives print readers the essential comparison without full 10-dimension sprawl.

3. **Remove from V2 §8.3.** Comparison Matrix doesn't belong in assessment volume. Replace §8.3 slot with something V2-native (or leave §8.1-8.2 as the full Advanced Reference section).

**Page impact on V1:** +2 pages (552 total). Within budget.
**Page impact on V2:** -12 pages (458 total). Leaner.
