**Release**: Structured Products Knowledge Ecosystem V1.0
**Date**: 2026-06-27

## Overview

Version 1.0 of the Structured Products Knowledge Ecosystem is a comprehensive, production-grade knowledge system covering 49 structured products across 6 product families. The ecosystem comprises 13 canonical content artifacts totaling ~41,426 lines, supported by 12 governance artifacts and a full release engineering package.

---

## What's Included

### Primary Deliverable
- **Desk Bible V2** — 25,764-line manuscript covering Parts 0-6, including all 49 product chapters with 22-section template compliance and a complete infrastructure encyclopedia (Part 6, §6.1-§6.11)

### Companion Artifacts
- **Infrastructure Encyclopedia V1.0** — ~347 entries covering conventions, legal, credit events, collateral, P&L, FTP/XVA, model risk, lifecycle, systems, and regulatory frameworks
- **Product DNA Atlas** — 49 product identity cards with 16 standardized fields each
- **Interview System V2.2** — 390 interview questions spanning 13 roles and 15 banks, with 3-tier difficulty calibration
- **Solutions Manual** — 40 structuring scenarios and 18 anti-patterns for practitioner decision-making

### Navigation & Reference
- **Product Universe Map** — Unified navigation integrating 3 graph systems
- **Product Comparison Matrix** — 12-dimension comparison across all 49 products
- **Learning Dependency Graph** — Prerequisite DAG with 5 tiers, 53 edges, 5 learning paths
- **Product Evolution Map** — 6 family evolution trees with 45 edges (35 within-family + 10 cross-family)
- **Complexity Registry** — Canonical complexity scores for all 49 products (scale 1-10, anchored at CDO=10)
- **Publication Taxonomy** — 6-dimension categorical classification system
- **Jargon Watchlist** — ~50 controlled vocabulary terms across 5 categories
