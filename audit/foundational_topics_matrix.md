### Content Available for Adaptation

`Structured Products.docx` is the richest source for foundational content. Its Sections A–F contain from-zero explanations of trading concepts, options, Greeks, volatility, hedging, and behavioral finance. The tone is conversational and suitable for new joiners — significantly better than V1's formal style.

`COMPLETE.docx` has the best structural design: Part 1 (Foundations), Part 2 (Four-Leg Framework), and Parts 4.3/7.3 (comparison tables). Its content is more concise but less detailed than `Structured Products.docx`.

### Content That Must Be Written

The 16 missing topics cluster in three areas:
1. **Options mechanics** (exercise styles, moneyness, intrinsic/time value) — fundamental prerequisites
2. **Product framework** (capital protection, product construction, lifecycles) — bridging concepts
3. **Operational infrastructure** (system overviews, centralized booking, fixing, settlement, reconciliation) — currently fragmented across 28 products
